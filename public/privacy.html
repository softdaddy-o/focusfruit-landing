Privacy Policy for FocusFruit

Effective Date: February 3, 2026
Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

FocusFruit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FocusFruit Chrome Extension.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only

FocusFruit stores all user data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We collect and store:

  • Timer Settings: Work duration, break duration, long break duration, auto-start preferences
  • Session History: Completed pomodoro sessions, timestamps, duration
  • Task Tags: Custom tags you create and assign to sessions
  • Productivity Statistics: Daily, weekly, and monthly analytics calculated from your sessions
  • Theme Preferences: Selected fruit theme and UI customization
  • Website Blocklist: Custom blocked websites and selected categories (Premium feature)
  • Sound Settings: Notification sound preferences and volume

Premium Account Data (Optional)

If you subscribe to FocusFruit Premium, we additionally collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and login via Supabase Authentication
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely via Supabase
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active premium subscription via Polar.sh
  • Payment Information: Processed by Polar.sh (we do not store credit card details)

How We Use Your Information

Local Data

  • Timer Functionality: To run the Pomodoro timer and track your focus sessions
  • Analytics: To calculate and display your productivity statistics
  • Personalization: To save your theme, tag, and notification preferences
  • Website Blocking: To block distracting websites during focus sessions (Premium)

Account Data

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and enable premium features
  • Subscription Management: To determine your subscription status and grant premium access
  • Support: To provide customer support if you contact us

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your data with advertisers
  • ❌ Track your browsing activity outside of the extension
  • ❌ Collect behavioral analytics
  • ❌ Use your data for marketing purposes unrelated to FocusFruit

We DO Share Data With:

  • Supabase: Authentication service for premium accounts (email, encrypted password)
  • Polar.sh: Payment processor for subscription management (email, subscription status)

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Polar.sh Privacy Policy: https://polar.sh/legal/privacy

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Data: Stored on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. Only you have access.
  • Premium Account Data: Stored on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Website Blocklist: Remains on your local device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by FocusFruit in Chrome's extension storage
  • Delete: Clear all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Export: Export your statistics (Premium feature)
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your premium account by contacting us

Permissions Explanation

FocusFruit requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Purpose
storage Store your settings, session history, tags, and statistics locally
alarms Run the Pomodoro timer accurately in the background
notifications Show desktop notifications when sessions start/end
tabs Enable website blocking during focus sessions (Premium)
idle Detect inactivity to pause the timer automatically
declarativeNetRequest Block distracting websites during work sessions (Premium)
offscreen Play notification sounds reliably in the background
host_permissions Required for website blocking feature (Premium)

Children's Privacy

FocusFruit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
